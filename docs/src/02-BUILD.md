# Project

### About UCollab
UCollab is an online collaboration platform designed specifically for University of Cincinnati students enrolled in Information Technology, Computer Science, Information Systems, and other tech related fields. The UCollab platform will allow UC students to connect, collaborate, and provide feedback to each other on code-based projects. This will help promote interdisciplinary knowledge sharing, allow for constructive assessment of work between peers, and present an environment for students that will provide them with a diverse range of networking opportunities.
